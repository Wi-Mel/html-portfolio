Mel's Best Movies & Series

Top Series

Prison Break

Excellent plot. Especially love the character of Scofield and his beautiful love story with Sara

Empire

Love, love, love Cookie❤️. She's a badass😎.
Sort of a musical, great songs. One of my all time favourite songs is 'Dream on With You'🎶🤩, sung by Lucious.

Narcos

Safe to say that I love films with some sort of drug trafficking involved. Why are they always sooo great?!
There's two of Narcos, one covers Colombia's drug lords like Escobar and the other Mexico's, and they are both lit👌!
The intro song, 'Tuyo', by Rodrigo Amarante is a masterpiece. It's got a charm to it I swear😂
Disclaimer❗❗These films lead to the strong urge to learn Espanol. Beautiful language👌. My favourite word's 'puta'🤭. You've gotta hear Escobar say it!
